# Privacy Policy for MX Xerox

Last updated: June 2026

MX Xerox is built as a fully functional printing utility. This privacy policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
We do not collect, store, use, or share any personal information, device data, or user files. 

* **No Data Collection:** The App does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).
* **100% Offline Processing:** All document and image processing required for printing happens entirely locally on your device. No data is ever transmitted over the internet or sent to external servers.
* **No Third-Party SDKs:** The App does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising software that could collect your data.

## 2. Device Permissions
The App may request access to your device's storage or local network permissions (such as finding local Wi-Fi printers). These permissions are used exclusively to allow you to select files for printing and send them directly to your printer. This data is never read or stored by us.

## 3. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
